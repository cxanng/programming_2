The game have been implemented with most functions needed

- All seven types of tetromino are included and they are all can be rotate by pressing R
- Tetrominos are movable horizontally by pressing A and D
- Tetrominos are stopped when hitting the bottom or on top of other tetrominos
- There is a label to inform user when the game is over
- If the current tetromino stopped and the new tetromino has not been created,
    it can still be moved horizontally or in some situations roteted
- Full rows are removed
- The game will add points everytime a row is removed, also the tetrominos will
    drop faster as the points increase (max = 300)
- Players can restart the game when the game is over or paused
- The next tetromino is displayed
- The current tetromino can be dropped as much as possible by pressing S
